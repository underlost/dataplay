Game Releases by Month

Nothing too surprising when we look at the number of new titles released month by month. You can see the summer slump of July holding the least amount of new titles with only 76 titles, and October seeing the most with 188. While that may sound like a lot, keep in mind we track numerous sources such as Ninteno Eshop, Steam Early access and various downloadable games are being tracked. App store games are not tracked, unless they're popular enough and have their sales reported. In short, if a game is released and can be purchased, it's most likely included in that month. All data is for the US region only.

New Releases, Sorted by Console

Wii Xbox 360 Playstation 3

When we break releases down by consoles (Xbox 360, Playstation 3, Wii), the trend changes slightly. Suddenly December is the month with fewest titles, and March becomes the second highest month. Wii releases were so few for 2012, I considered not even including it. But on the other hand, I thought it was interesting to see just how few games came out. Diminishing sales of the Wii console and the upcoming Wii-U release most likely lead to the small amount of games released for 2012.

Sales (In Millions) Over the Past 7 Years

Wii Xbox 360 Playstation 3

This generation of consoles kicked off in 2005 with the release of the Xbox 350. While the Playstation 3 started out slow because of it's price tag, byt 2009 it caught up to the 360. Both remain to have steady sales well into 2013. The Wii on the other hand peaked in 2008 with a whopping 24.18 million units sold, only to fall year after year. Despite it's decline, the Wii still had a total of 98.6 million units sold overall, compared to the Xbox 360's 74.8 million, and Playstation 3's 73.8 million.

Global Hardware Sales (In Millions)

While Nintendo's home consoles arent doing as well as well as the PS3 or 360, they make up for it in hand held sales. Even though analaysts were unsure of the fate of hand held consoles being replaced by the smart phone, the 3DS shows there is still room in the hand held marlet with 14.41 million units sold world wide. That's almost 2 million more than the Playstation 3 at 12.73 million.

Popular Trends

PC Xbox 360 Playstation 3

Themes

Popular themes of 2012 is mostly what I expected. The majority of games released on PC, Playstation 3 and Xbox 360 were fantasy and scifi. The Playstation had a surprising amount of anime themed games. Xbox 30 lead in military titles. One thing to keep in mind when looking at this chart, games ca have more then one theme, so it's somewhat hard to come up with exact numbers, therefore percentages are used. For example, a game can have fantasy elements, yet still be a anime game. A game could be militaristic, but also have SciFi elements to it as well.

Genres

Nothing too surprising with the genres for 2012. The amount of shooters on all platforms were nearly identical. The PC continues to be the platform of choice for sim games, while sports and fighting games continue to ship mostly to consoles. The only real surprise to me was the amount of RPGS that came out on PC compared to consoles. Again, just as themes, genres can overlap. Games like Diablo III can be considered both an Action and RPG game.
